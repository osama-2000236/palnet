## Community Guidelines

Baydar is for professional identity, work, hiring, learning, and constructive public conversation.

## Be Real

Use your real professional identity. Do not impersonate people, companies, recruiters, or public institutions.

## Be Respectful

Do not harass, threaten, shame, dox, or target people based on protected characteristics, politics, religion, nationality, gender, disability, or background.

## Keep It Useful

Do not post spam, scams, fake jobs, malware, engagement manipulation, or repeated low-quality content.

## Report Problems

Use the report tools when content or behavior breaks these guidelines. Reports are reviewed by moderators and may lead to takedowns, suspensions, blocks, appeals, and audit records.
