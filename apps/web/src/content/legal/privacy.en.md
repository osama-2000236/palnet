## Privacy Policy

Baydar collects the information needed to run a professional network: account details, profile data, posts, comments, messages, connections, job activity, device metadata, and moderation records.

## How We Use Data

We use data to provide the service, secure accounts, deliver notifications, process job workflows, moderate abuse, improve reliability, and comply with legal duties.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share data with service providers only as needed to operate Baydar, including hosting, storage, email, push notifications, analytics, and error monitoring.

## Your Choices

You can update your profile, manage notification preferences, block users, request an account export, and request deletion from account settings.

## Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes above, legal obligations, dispute handling, security, and audit trails.
